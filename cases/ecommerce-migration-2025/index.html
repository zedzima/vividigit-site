The Challenge

Traffic Loss

40% drop in organic traffic within 2 weeks of migration. Product pages lost rankings across all major keywords.

Broken Redirects

Over 15,000 URLs changed during migration. Many redirect chains, loops, and 404 errors were discovered.

Schema Loss

Product structured data was not migrated to the new platform, causing rich snippet loss in search results.

process

What We Did

Emergency Audit

Full crawl of old and new site to identify all broken URLs, redirect issues, and missing pages.

Redirect Mapping

Created comprehensive redirect map for 15,000+ URLs. Fixed chains, loops, and 404s.

Schema Recovery

Reimplemented product schema markup on the new platform with enhanced structured data.

Monitoring

Weekly tracking of crawl stats, indexing, and rankings. Iterative fixes based on data.
