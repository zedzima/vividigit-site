Real Alconost client, last 4 months. Three measured channels: Organic, Paid Search, Paid Shopping. Organic delivered the most revenue (€107,614 · 41% share) — more than Paid Shopping (€103,974) and over 2× Paid Search (€48,640) — on the smallest investment (€8K of SEO work). The compounding effect: every additional organic visit lowers the average cost per visit, while every paid click still costs the same. PPC buys traffic now; SEO buys a compounding asset. Both belong in the mix — PPC to hit the 6-month KPI, SEO so you're not paying premium CPCs forever.
