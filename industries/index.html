WHY INDUSTRY MATTERS

Industry expertise changes what works and what wastes money

Buyer journeys, compliance rules, competitive dynamics, and content expectations vary by vertical. Marketing that ignores this produces generic results.

SaaS buyers evaluate differently than e-commerce shoppers

A SaaS buyer reads comparison pages, watches demos, and checks G2 reviews before contacting sales. An e-commerce shopper searches product terms, compares prices, and decides in minutes. The same SEO, PPC, or content strategy does not work for both.

1

Regulated industries need compliant marketing, not just creative marketing

Fintech and healthcare have compliance rules that disqualify most agency content on the first draft. Industry expertise means producing work that passes legal review without losing its marketing effectiveness.

2

Competitive dynamics shape which services matter most

In saturated SaaS markets, product positioning and content authority drive growth. In local healthcare, reputation management and local SEO dominate. Industry experience tells us which services to prioritize before spending your budget discovering it.
