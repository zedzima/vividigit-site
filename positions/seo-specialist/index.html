Common Questions

About this position

Is this a remote position?

Yes. We're a remote-first team. You can work from anywhere, though we prefer candidates in European time zones (UTC-1 to UTC+3) for easier collaboration with the team and clients.

What tools do you use?

Google Search Console, Ahrefs, Screaming Frog, Semrush, Surfer SEO, and custom internal tools. You'll have full access to all premium subscriptions from day one.

What's the team structure?

You'll report to the Head of SEO and work alongside other SEO specialists, content writers, and developers. Each specialist manages their own client projects with support from the team.

Is there room for growth?

Absolutely. We're growing fast and believe in promoting from within. Senior Specialist, Team Lead, and Head of Department tracks are all open and well-defined.
