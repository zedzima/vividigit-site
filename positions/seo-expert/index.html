ROLE OVERVIEW

What You Will Actually Do

A senior SEO role focused on real market-level execution, not generic international strategy.

Your responsibilities

You take ownership of defined SEO work inside a specific market or language context:

  • market-specific keyword research and SERP review
  • localization-aware on-page recommendations
  • technical audit input where local search behavior matters
  • competitor review for the target market
  • collaboration with the wider team through shared quality standards and workflows

What we need from you

  • strong hands-on SEO experience
  • real familiarity with at least one market, language cluster, or buyer context
  • ability to work inside a standardized delivery system
  • readiness to own outcomes for your lane, not just execute isolated tasks

What this role is not

This is not a generalist “do all international SEO” position. We need one strong executor per market, working within a shared system. You bring market depth. The system handles coordination.

FAQ

Questions SEO experts usually ask

About this role and the operating model

Is this a full-time role?

Usually no. This role is designed as project-based or part-time expert work. Some experts become deeply involved over time, but the model starts from clear scopes and real delivery needs rather than default full-time staffing.

Do I need country or language specialization to be a strong fit?

Yes, usually. General SEO capability matters, but the strongest fit is an expert who can pair the craft with real familiarity in one specific market, language, or both.

What tools and systems do you use?

The stack varies by scope, but commonly includes Google Search Console, GA4, Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, Semrush, technical crawlers, localization workflows, and internal SOPs for quality control and delivery structure.

How does compensation work?

Core compensation is a 40% revenue share on the revenue your work generates. Final payout depends on scope, rate, delivered units, and contribution. On top of that, 20% of company profit is distributed as annual bonus based on contribution. Key contributors also receive phantom equity for long-term upside. Exact terms depend on the scope, market complexity, and engagement model.

Is this an international strategist role?

Usually no. In many cases this role is the market executor inside a broader multi-market system. We still need cross-market coordination, but delivery quality depends on having someone who really understands the local market.

Can I apply if I do not match every listed market?

Yes. If you are especially strong in one market, one language cluster, or one kind of SEO scope, that can still be highly relevant. The goal is not generic coverage; it is strong, specific expertise.
