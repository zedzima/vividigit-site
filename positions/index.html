FAQ

Questions experts ask before joining

Is this a full-time position or contract work?

Roles are project-based within the Elastic Talent Model. You work on real client engagements with clear scope and ownership. The model is flexible — some experts work exclusively with us, others balance multiple commitments.

Can I work remotely?

Yes. The model is remote-first. Experts work from wherever they are most productive, with coordination handled through shared tools and async processes.

What kind of projects will I work on?

Productized scopes of work for international clients across SaaS, e-commerce, fintech, healthcare, and other verticals. The work is structured, the deliverables are defined, and the quality standards are explicit.

How does compensation work?

Compensation is tied to scope delivery, not hours. Rates reflect seniority, domain expertise, and the value of the deliverables. The structure is transparent and discussed before any engagement begins.
