ROLE OVERVIEW

What You Will Actually Do

Market-level ASO execution across iOS and Android.

Responsibilities

You take ownership of defined ASO work inside specific markets and platforms.

  • Keyword research Market-specific keyword analysis, semantic core building, and search demand mapping for App Store and Google Play
  • Metadata optimization Title, subtitle, keyword field, description, and tag optimization — adapted per platform and locale
  • Competitor analysis Regular monitoring of competitor keyword strategies, store page positioning, and ranking changes
  • Localization Native-quality ASO localization for target markets — keyword research, metadata, and messaging adaptation per locale
  • Store page conversion Screenshot strategy briefs, A/B test hypothesis design, icon testing recommendations, and Custom Product Page strategy
  • Reporting Ranking tracking, conversion monitoring, and performance reporting through shared dashboards

Requirements

What we need from you to be effective in this role.

  • ASO experience 2+ years of hands-on app store optimization for iOS or Android — keyword research, metadata, A/B testing
  • Platform knowledge Understanding of App Store and Google Play ranking algorithms, metadata rules, and optimization techniques
  • Tools proficiency Confident with AppTweak, Sensor Tower, Data.ai, Google Play Console, or App Store Connect
  • Analytical mindset Data-driven approach to keyword selection, A/B testing, and performance optimization
  • Communication Clear written communication in English; additional languages are a strong advantage for multilingual ASO

Nice to have

Not required, but these make you a stronger fit.

  • Multiple markets ASO experience across more than one country or language
  • Apple Search Ads Hands-on experience with Apple Search Ads setup and management
  • Creative direction Ability to write screenshot copy, design A/B test hypotheses, and brief designers for store page visuals
  • Complex languages Experience with ASO for Japanese, Korean, Chinese, or Arabic markets

STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

Common Questions About ASO Specialist Jobs at Vividigit

How the role works, what to expect, and how compensation is structured.

How do ASO specialists get matched to apps and clients?

When a client engagement requires your platform or market expertise, you get matched to a defined ASO scope. The system tracks which specialists cover which platforms, markets, and categories. You can work on multiple apps across different clients simultaneously — which is how earnings scale.

What does a typical work week look like for an ASO specialist here?

It depends on active scopes. Some specialists work 10 hours per week on one app. Others work 25+ hours across several. The work is structured: you receive a scope with clear deliverables, use shared templates, and submit through quality gates. Most ASO specialists say they spend more time on actual optimization here than in any previous role.

What tools do ASO specialists use?

AppTweak, Sensor Tower, Data.ai, Google Play Console, App Store Connect, and attribution tools like AppsFlyer or Adjust. The system provides shared keyword research templates and reporting frameworks. You bring your ASO judgment. The system provides the operational layer.

What is the salary range for freelance ASO specialists?

There is no fixed ASO specialist salary. Earnings are tied to revenue share — 40% of the revenue your work generates. An ASO specialist working across multiple apps and markets earns based on output, not salary bands. On top of revenue share, 20% of company profit is distributed annually.

Do I need experience with both iOS and Android?

Strong expertise in one platform is enough. If you are deep in App Store optimization or Google Play — that is valuable. Specialists who cover both platforms have access to more scopes, but single-platform expertise is a valid starting point.

Is multilingual ASO experience required?

Not required, but it is a strong advantage. International ASO across multiple languages opens significantly more scopes. If you can do native keyword research in German, French, Spanish, or other languages — that multiplies your earning potential.

Can I apply if I also do Apple Search Ads?

Absolutely. ASO and Apple Search Ads share keyword intelligence and creative data. Specialists who can own both ASO and ASA scopes are especially valuable because the two disciplines reinforce each other. We welcome cross-functional expertise.
