FAQ

Questions experts ask before joining

Is this a full-time position or contract work?

Most roles are project-based, part-time, or fractional by design. We match experts to defined scopes and market-specific workstreams rather than defaulting to traditional full-time staffing. Some roles grow into ongoing retainer relationships.

Can I work remotely?

Yes. The operating model is remote-first. What matters more than office presence is whether your expertise, time zone overlap, language ability, and delivery discipline fit the work.

How does compensation actually work?

Core compensation is a 40% revenue share on the revenue your work generates. Final payout depends on scope, rate, delivered units, and contribution. On top of that, 20% of company profit is distributed as annual bonus based on contribution. Key contributors also receive phantom equity for long-term upside.

What kinds of experts are you looking for?

Service leads, scope experts, country and language managers, and industry-specific operators. If you can own a capability in a specific market, language, or vertical, there is a strong chance your role belongs in the network.

Do I need to match an exact listed vacancy?

No. If your expertise clearly maps to one of our services, scopes, countries, languages, or industries, reach out even if your exact role is not listed yet. The system is designed to keep expanding with the delivery architecture.

Why work through this model instead of a normal in-house role?

Because the model is built for experts who want more time on diagnosis, strategy, and meaningful execution. Less time on internal politics, admin overhead, sales noise, and repeatedly rebuilding the same operating logic from zero. Plus, your earnings are directly tied to value created, not to salary bands.
