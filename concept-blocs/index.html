DIGITAL MARKETING SYSTEM

Eight disciplines. One governed system.

Each discipline answers a different strategic question, uses different tools, and is measured by different KPIs. Vividigit operates across all of them inside one productized model.

Search Marketing

Organic visibility wherever intent exists. SEO, AEO, and GEO are converging — the same core skills now apply across Google, Bing, ChatGPT, Perplexity, voice assistants, and any future discovery surface.

Focus
Organic visibility across search engines, AI answer engines, and discovery platforms
Tools & Methods
Demand research, semantic architecture, technical audits, on-page optimization, link building, entity SEO, structured data, AI citation analysis
Key Tasks
Search demand mapping, site architecture, technical SEO, content optimization, link acquisition, AI visibility audits, local and international optimization
KPIs
Organic traffic, keyword rankings, search visibility share, AI citation rate, organic CTR, domain authority

The question: Are we visible where intent exists — regardless of the search interface?

Growth Marketing

A methodology oriented around the full customer lifecycle — not just acquisition, but activation, retention, virality, and revenue.

Focus
Full-funnel optimization: Acquisition → Activation → Retention → Revenue → Referral
Tools & Methods
A/B testing platforms, product analytics (Mixpanel, Amplitude), cohort analysis, rapid experimentation frameworks
Key Tasks
Identify growth levers, test hypotheses fast, optimize the conversion funnel, reduce customer acquisition cost
KPIs
CAC, LTV, activation rate, retention rate, viral coefficient

The question: What moves the needle?

Performance Marketing

Strictly tied to financial return. Every dollar spent must be traceable to a specific action and revenue outcome.

Focus
Paid channels with payment tied to measurable actions — sale, registration, lead
Tools & Methods
Google Ads, Meta Ads, programmatic DSPs, attribution platforms, bid management tools
Key Tasks
Buy traffic that pays for itself, optimize ROAS in real time, scale profitable campaigns
KPIs
ROAS, ROI, CPA, CPL, conversion rate, revenue per click

The question: Does this traffic pay for itself right now?

Community Marketing

Attracting and retaining audiences through value, not interruption. Building trust before the sale.

Focus
Expert content, brand communities, audience loyalty through useful information
Tools & Methods
Blogs, YouTube, podcasts, Telegram channels, newsletters, community platforms
Key Tasks
Build authority through expertise, create content that attracts organically, nurture community engagement
KPIs
Organic traffic, engagement rate, subscriber growth, content-attributed pipeline, community activity

The question: What value do we provide before the sale?

Product Marketing

Where other disciplines find the channels, Product Marketing defines the message.

Focus
Positioning, competitive analysis, Go-to-Market strategy, sales enablement
Tools & Methods
Customer research, competitive intelligence tools, messaging frameworks, feature launch playbooks
Key Tasks
Define what we sell, to whom, and why they should buy. Launch new features. Train and enable sales teams.
KPIs
Win rate, feature adoption, competitive displacement, sales cycle length, message-market fit

Brand Marketing

The long game. Unlike Growth Marketing measured in real-time metrics, Brand Marketing builds emotional connection and recognition over time.

Tools & Methods
PR campaigns, image advertising, brand guidelines, sponsorships, storytelling
Key Tasks
Create emotional connection with the audience, build recognition, establish trust at scale
KPIs
Brand awareness, brand recall, NPS, share of voice, sentiment score

The question: How do people feel about the brand?

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from hiring freelancers?

You do not manage a loose set of experts yourself. You buy a governed delivery system: scope, staffing, QA, reporting, and accountability in one model. We handle coordination, quality gates, and cross-functional alignment.

Do you replace an in-house team?

Usually no. Our strongest fit is as a high-quality extension of an in-house marketing or growth team. Your team keeps strategic ownership of the business while we add expert depth, multilingual execution, and controlled delivery.

How does the productized model work?

You can start with a diagnostic scope, a service, or a solution. We define deliverables, timeline, success logic, and ownership up front, then assemble the right experts inside one governed operating model. No hourly sprawl, no hidden staffing layer.

Can you work across multiple countries and languages?

That is one of our core strengths. Our system is built around cross-border operating logic: shared architecture, local adaptation, and multilingual execution without losing strategic control or fragmenting delivery.

How do you use AI?

AI accelerates research, pattern recognition, and production. It never replaces strategy, judgment, or quality control. Every external-facing asset passes through human review, and every project operates within clear AI governance boundaries.

What's the best way to start?

The fastest entry point is usually a diagnostic audit: a defined, low-risk first step that delivers clarity and naturally leads into a broader scope if there is a strong fit. You can also browse services or start from a solution if the problem is already well defined.
