STILL HAVE QUESTIONS?

Questions about digital marketing consulting

What consulting includes, how it works, and when it makes sense.

How much does digital marketing consulting cost?

A focused diagnostic or audit engagement starts at a few thousand dollars. A comprehensive consulting engagement — covering multiple markets, channels, and capabilities — scales with scope. Every engagement is priced upfront as a productized package, so there are no hourly billing surprises. We define the cost before starting, based on what you need diagnosed and delivered.

What is the difference between consulting and strategy?

Consulting is broader: it can include market audits, measurement reviews, vendor assessments, and organizational recommendations — not just a marketing plan. Strategy is one possible output of consulting. Some clients need a strategy. Others need an honest assessment of what is not working and why. The consulting engagement is scoped to what you actually need, not to a standard template.

Can I hire a digital marketing consultant without committing to execution?

Yes. Consulting is offered as a standalone engagement. You get diagnosis, analysis, and recommendations — then decide what to execute and with whom. Many clients start with consulting to validate priorities, then move to productized execution services when they are ready. There is no lock-in.

What kind of digital marketing consultant works at Vividigit?

Our consultants are senior practitioners with hands-on experience across SEO, analytics, content, PPC, and international marketing. They have built and operated digital marketing systems for multilingual and global brands — not just advised on them. Every consultant is a named expert with a visible track record, not a rotating junior associate.

How is this different from a typical marketing consultancy?

Most consultancies sell time. We sell defined outcomes. Every consulting engagement is productized: fixed scope, clear deliverables, agreed timeline, transparent pricing. We also bring multilingual and cross-market intelligence that generalist consultancies cannot — because we operate across multiple languages and have delivery infrastructure, not just advisory capacity.

Can you consult on international or multilingual marketing specifically?

That is a core strength. Many consulting engagements focus specifically on international expansion: which markets to enter, how to adapt strategy per country, what the competitive landscape looks like in each language, and how to measure across geographies. We bring native-language market intelligence, not translated assumptions.

How long does a consulting engagement take?

A focused audit or diagnostic typically takes 2-4 weeks. A comprehensive consulting engagement covering multiple markets and capabilities takes 4-8 weeks. The timeline is defined upfront as part of scoping, and you receive actionable output at each stage — not just a final report at the end.
