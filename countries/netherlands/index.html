TEXT

Digital Marketing in the Netherlands for International Companies

The Netherlands is one of Europe's most digitally mature markets. E-commerce penetration is among the highest in the EU, mobile commerce is standard, and Dutch consumers are pragmatic, research-driven buyers. English fluency is exceptional — 90% of the population uses English — but that does not make English marketing the right default. Native Dutch content consistently outperforms on trust, conversion, and SEO, especially in regulated sectors and higher-consideration B2B. We are not a local Dutch agency competing for Dutch clients. We are an outsourced, productized marketing services agency for companies based outside the Netherlands that need international expansion into the Dutch market without building an Amsterdam office. Our multilingual, international marketing model pairs native Dutch experts with shared operating standards, so global brands get Dutch-level quality across Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague, Utrecht, and Eindhoven. Expert digital marketing in the Netherlands, delivered as a productized service — from market-entry research and native Dutch content to ongoing growth across the Randstad and beyond.

FAQ

Digital marketing in the Netherlands — common questions

Do you work with international companies expanding into the Netherlands?

Yes — that is our primary fit. Vividigit is an outsourced, productized marketing services agency for brands based outside the Netherlands that need native Dutch reach. Local Dutch agencies serve Dutch clients well; we serve international companies that need an expert, multilingual partner for the Dutch market without setting up an Amsterdam office.

Can we just run English campaigns given the high English fluency in the Netherlands?

You can — and it often makes sense as a starting point, because English fluency in the Netherlands is extraordinarily high. But for SEO, long-form content, trust-sensitive categories, and B2B nurture, native Dutch consistently outperforms English. The best setup is usually English first, Dutch layered on where it genuinely lifts performance.

Can localization and translation be a faster, cheaper first step before full digital marketing in the Netherlands?

Often yes. Before scaling into SEO, content, and paid campaigns, Dutch-language localization of your site and core assets is the lowest-cost way to test where native Dutch lifts performance over English. We cover this through our Alconost heritage — 20+ years of professional localization and translation across 100+ languages — so you can sequence Dutch localization first, then scale into full digital marketing services in the Netherlands once the signal is positive.

Which Dutch cities do you cover?

Across the Netherlands, including Amsterdam, Rotterdam, The Hague, Utrecht, Eindhoven, and wider Randstad. Productized digital marketing in the Netherlands does not require a local office — native Dutch specialists work across the country with market-specific targeting, and our delivery extends into Flemish Belgium when full Benelux coverage is needed.
