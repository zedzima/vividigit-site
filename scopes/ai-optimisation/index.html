WHY IT MATTERS

Why AI Visibility Matters Now

AI Is Changing How People Find Information

Millions of users now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude instead of typing queries into Google. These AI tools don't return a list of links — they synthesize a single answer from sources they trust. If your content isn't structured for AI consumption, you're invisible in this new channel regardless of your Google rankings.

1

What Happens When AI Ignores Your Brand

When a potential customer asks an AI tool about your industry, the response cites your competitors — not you. Every unanswered query is a lost opportunity where AI actively directs attention elsewhere. Unlike search rankings where you still appear on page two, in AI answers you either get cited or you don't exist.

2

The First-Mover Advantage Is Real

AI models form preferences based on content quality, structure, and authority signals that exist today. Brands that optimize now are training the next generation of models to recognize them as authoritative sources. Waiting means your competitors lock in citation patterns that become increasingly difficult to displace.

Key Focus Areas

Where AI visibility is won or lost

LLM Citation Patterns

We reverse-engineer how large language models select sources for their answers. By analyzing citation patterns across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude, we identify exactly what makes content citable — and apply those patterns to yours.

Entity & Knowledge Graph

AI tools rely on structured knowledge to identify authoritative sources. We build and strengthen your entity presence across knowledge graphs, ensuring AI models recognize your brand, products, and expertise as distinct, trustworthy entities.

Content Structure

AI models extract information differently than search crawlers. We restructure your content with clear definitions, direct answers, and logical hierarchies that make it easy for AI to parse, understand, and cite in generated responses.

AI Monitoring & Tracking

Visibility in AI answers changes with every model update. We continuously monitor how AI tools reference your content, track citation frequency across platforms, and adjust strategy based on measurable shifts in AI-generated mentions.

OUR APPROACH

Our Approach to AI Optimisation

Data-Driven Auditing

Every engagement starts with evidence, not assumptions. We query AI tools with hundreds of industry-relevant prompts, catalog which sources get cited and why, and map the competitive landscape before making a single recommendation.

Iterative Optimization

AI models update frequently, and citation patterns shift with each update. We don't deliver a one-time report and disappear. Our process is built around cycles: implement changes, measure impact after the next model update, refine the approach, and repeat.

Cross-Platform Monitoring

Your audience uses different AI tools — ChatGPT for general queries, Perplexity for research, Claude for analysis. Each model has different citation preferences. We monitor your visibility across all major platforms and tailor recommendations for each one.

FAQ

Common Questions

Practical answers about working with us

How long does the AI Visibility Audit take?

Depends on the scope. S tier (20 queries) takes 5-7 business days. M tier (50 queries) takes 7-10 days. L tier (100 queries) takes 10-15 days. XL is custom-scoped.

What do I get after the audit?

A detailed report with: an AI landscape map showing how AI explains your industry, citation analysis identifying which sources get cited and why, a gap report highlighting what's missing from your content, and a prioritized action plan with estimated impact.

Which AI tools do you analyze?

We focus on the major LLMs that include citations: ChatGPT (with browsing), Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot. The specific mix depends on what's relevant for your market and audience.

Do you implement the changes or just report them?

The audit delivers a report with prioritized recommendations. Content optimization and entity SEO can be ordered as separate tasks. You can also implement the recommendations with your own team — the report includes everything they need.

Can we start with a small scope and expand later?

Yes, that's exactly what the tier system is for. Start with the S tier audit (20 queries, $500) to validate the approach, then expand to more queries or add content optimization tasks based on the findings.
