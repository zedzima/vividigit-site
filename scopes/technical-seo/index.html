Why Technical SEO Matters

Invisible Issues Kill Rankings

Most technical SEO problems are invisible to the naked eye. Crawl traps, orphaned pages, duplicate content, broken canonical chains — these issues silently erode your search visibility while your content team keeps publishing into a broken foundation. By the time rankings drop noticeably, the damage has compounded across hundreds of pages.
1

Business Impact Is Real and Measurable

A single misconfigured robots.txt can de-index your entire product catalog overnight. Slow Core Web Vitals push you below competitors in mobile search. Missing hreflang tags mean your German customers see English pages while your English content cannibalizes the German version. Every technical gap translates directly into lost traffic, lost revenue, and wasted content investment.
2

When You Need a Professional Audit

If your site has more than 500 pages, recently migrated platforms, serves multiple languages, or has seen unexplained ranking drops — a professional audit is not optional. Free tools catch surface-level issues. A specialist finds the structural problems that free tools miss entirely: JavaScript rendering failures, faceted navigation bloat, internal PageRank leaks, and indexation logic errors that only show up at scale.
3

What We Specialize In

Deep expertise in critical technical areas

Large-Scale Site Audits

Auditing sites with 10,000 to 500,000+ pages requires a different methodology than small-site crawls. We use log file analysis, segmented crawling, and statistical sampling to find patterns that per-page tools miss entirely.

Platform Migrations

Moving from Magento to Shopify, WordPress to headless, or any re-platforming scenario. We handle redirect mapping, URL taxonomy design, and staged rollout plans that preserve your organic equity through the transition.

Schema & Structured Data

From basic Organization and FAQ markup to complex Product, Review, and Event schemas. We implement structured data that earns rich results and feeds AI-powered search features like Google SGE and Bing Chat.

Core Web Vitals Optimization

LCP, INP, and CLS audits with actionable fix plans. We diagnose performance bottlenecks at the code level — render-blocking resources, layout shifts, heavy JavaScript bundles — and deliver specific optimization steps.

approach

Our Approach to Technical SEO

Evidence-Based

We do not run a generic 50-point checklist and hand you a PDF. Every audit starts with your specific business goals, traffic patterns, and technical stack. We cross-reference crawl data with Google Search Console, server logs, and analytics to distinguish real problems from noise.

Industry-Grade Tooling

Our audits combine Screaming Frog, Sitebulb, Ahrefs, and custom Python scripts for log file analysis and large-scale data processing. For JavaScript-heavy sites, we use headless Chrome rendering to see exactly what Googlebot sees. No single tool catches everything — our value is in combining multiple data sources.

Transparent Reporting

Every deliverable comes with clear severity ratings, effort estimates, and expected outcomes. Your development team gets implementation-ready tickets, not vague suggestions. After delivery, we walk you through the findings in a live session and remain available for questions during implementation.
