WHY LOCAL SEO MATTERS

Local Visibility Drives Foot Traffic and Calls

When customers search for services near them, the businesses that appear in the local pack win the click. Everything else is invisible.

The local pack captures the majority of clicks

Google shows local results above organic listings for location-intent queries. If your business is not in the local pack, competitors take those calls, visits, and bookings. Ranking organically is not enough when a map with three pins sits above your link.

1

Inconsistent data erodes trust and rankings

Wrong phone numbers, outdated addresses, and duplicate listings confuse both customers and search engines. Google cross-references your business information across hundreds of sources. Every inconsistency weakens your local authority and pushes you down in results.

2

Multi-location businesses need a system, not manual effort

Managing five or fifty locations manually leads to missed updates, stale profiles, and uneven performance. A structured local SEO program ensures every location gets consistent attention, accurate data, and measurable results each month.

3

LOCAL SEO FAQ

Common Questions About Local SEO

Straight answers about scope, pricing, and delivery.

How long does a local SEO audit take?

S tier (1 location): 5-7 business days. M tier (5 locations): 7-10 days. L tier (15 locations): 10-15 days. XL is custom-scoped based on the number of locations and markets.

How much does a local SEO audit cost?

Audits start at $400 for a single location. Multi-location pricing: $1,000 (5 locations), $2,500 (15 locations), custom for 50+. Every tier includes the same deliverable quality — the price reflects the number of locations analyzed.

Do you work with businesses that have multiple locations?

Yes. Multi-location local SEO is a core strength. We manage businesses with anywhere from one location to fifty or more, with per-location tracking, reporting, and optimization.

What is included in Google Business Profile optimization?

Complete profile setup or optimization: category and attribute selection, photo and media strategy, post scheduling, Q&A seeding, service catalog setup, and a first month of GBP post content. Starts at $250 per location.

How do you build local citations?

Manual submissions to high-authority directories and data aggregators. We audit existing citations for NAP consistency, suppress duplicates, and provide a tracking spreadsheet with live URLs for every submission.

What does ongoing local SEO management include?

Monthly GBP posting, citation monitoring and corrections, review response support, local ranking tracking per location, and a monthly strategy call. Starts at $400 per month for a single location.

Do you handle review management?

Yes. We set up review generation funnels, create response template libraries, configure monitoring dashboards, and establish negative review escalation processes. We also provide staff training documentation for handling reviews internally.

What is the best first step?

A Local SEO Audit at the S tier ($400). It covers one location and gives you a complete picture of your local visibility, GBP health, citation accuracy, and competitive positioning — with a prioritized action plan. No ongoing commitment required.
