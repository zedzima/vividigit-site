WHY ASO OPTIMIZATION MATTERS

App Store Search Drives Installs — If Your Optimization Works

Over 65% of downloads come from app store search. Keyword gaps, weak metadata, and wrong category placement cost you installs every day.

Keywords you are missing drive your competitors' growth

Most apps are optimized for their own brand name — not for the search terms their users actually type. Multilingual ASO optimization starts with native keyword research per market, not translation of English keywords.

1

Metadata is the highest-leverage change you can make

Updating title, subtitle, and keyword field can shift rankings within days. International ASO optimization means separate metadata strategies per store and per locale — not one version pasted everywhere.

2

Category placement determines who can find you

Apps in the wrong category miss entire discovery surfaces. Category ranking is easier to win than search ranking — but only if you are in the right category. Global ASO optimization includes category audit for every market.

3

Ratings kill conversion before the install

Users see your rating before they read your description. A 3.7 average suppresses installs even with perfect keywords. Ratings and review strategy is a revenue lever — not a vanity metric.

ASO OPTIMIZATION FAQ

Common Questions About ASO Optimization

How much does ASO optimization cost?

ASO optimization tasks start at $200 per task per platform. Keyword research starts at $300 for App Store, $400 for Google Play, or $600 for both. A full single-market optimization package (keyword research + metadata + competitor analysis) starts at $700 for one store.

How long does ASO optimization take?

Individual tasks are delivered in 5-7 business days. A full ASO optimization package takes 7-10 days. Metadata updates can go live immediately after delivery — ranking changes typically become visible within 2-4 weeks.

What is the difference between App Store and Google Play optimization?

App Store uses a hidden keyword field and weighs the subtitle heavily. Google Play indexes the full description and uses tags. Ranking factors, metadata structures, and update cycles differ. We optimize each store separately — not with one approach applied to both.

Do you support multilingual ASO optimization?

Yes. Multilingual ASO optimization is a core capability. We perform native keyword research and metadata writing per locale — not machine translation of English listings. International ASO optimization across multiple languages with native specialists.

What are In-App Events and why do they matter?

In-App Events are an App Store-exclusive feature that promotes time-limited activities directly in search results and the Today tab. Well-executed events improve discoverability, boost installs, and re-engage existing users. Available on iOS only.
