WHY APP STORE CRO MATTERS

You Pay for Every Impression — Make Sure They Convert

Store page conversion directly determines the efficiency of every channel you invest in. Same traffic, more installs — the highest-ROI work in app marketing.

Your store page is losing installs right now

The average app store conversion rate is 3-5%. The best apps in competitive categories hit 10-15%. That gap is entirely store page CRO. Multilingual app store CRO means closing that gap in every language your users search in.

1

Screenshots are where conversion happens

Screenshots are the first visual element users engage with before reading a word of copy. Messaging hierarchy, value proposition sequencing, and text overlay strategy determine whether a user clicks Install or scrolls past. International app store CRO adapts this for every market.

2

Icons and visuals drive tap-through rates

Your app icon appears in every search result and category listing. A systematic A/B testing program for icons can improve tap-through rate by 20-40% — which improves conversion rate and, over time, ranking. Global app store CRO treats icon testing as a continuous optimization program.

3

Custom Product Pages unlock higher relevance

App Store CPPs let you show different store page variants to users from Apple Search Ads, Apple Emails, or custom links. Relevant store pages convert better than generic ones. CPPs are the bridge between your paid campaigns and your store listing.

APP STORE CRO FAQ

Common Questions About App Store CRO

How much does App Store CRO cost?

A single store listing A/B test or icon test starts at $160 per platform. Screenshot strategy starts at $300 per store. Custom Product Pages start at $600 for one CPP or $1,500 for three. Preview video concepts start at $400 per store.

How long does App Store CRO take?

Individual deliverables (strategy, brief, test setup) are completed in 5-7 business days. A/B tests run for 2-4 weeks depending on your traffic volume and statistical significance requirements. We provide a test duration estimate before starting.

What is a Custom Product Page and who should use it?

Custom Product Pages (App Store only) let you create alternative store listing variants for specific traffic sources — paid campaigns, Apple email links, or custom URLs. Apps running Apple Search Ads or tracking campaign-specific conversion should use CPPs to align store page messaging with ad creative.

What is Product Page Optimization?

Product Page Optimization (App Store) and Store Listing Experiments (Google Play) are the platforms' native A/B testing tools. They serve different store page variants to subsets of your organic traffic and measure conversion difference. We design tests and interpret results — the platforms handle statistical randomization.

Do you support multilingual app store CRO?

Yes. International app store CRO is a core capability. We create per-locale screenshot strategies, localized text overlays, and market-adapted messaging. Multilingual app store CRO across multiple languages with cultural adaptation.

How does App Store CRO relate to ASO?

ASO improves keyword rankings — it drives more traffic to your store page. App Store CRO converts more of that traffic into installs. Both improve organic install volume. A higher-converting store page also improves ranking signals, which feeds back into ASO.

What is the best first step?

A Store Listing A/B Test or Icon A/B Test — starting at $160. One structured experiment that establishes your conversion baseline and tests your highest-impact hypothesis. Delivers in one week, results visible in 2-3 weeks of test runtime.
