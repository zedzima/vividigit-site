Common Questions

Practical answers about social media marketing

Which social media platforms should my business be on?

It depends on your audience and goals. B2B companies typically see the best results on LinkedIn and X. E-commerce brands perform well on Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest. We analyze your target audience demographics and competitor presence to recommend the right platform mix — most businesses do not need to be everywhere.

How often should we post on social media?

Quality beats quantity. For most brands, 3-5 posts per week on primary platforms delivers strong results. We build content calendars based on platform best practices and your audience engagement patterns, then adjust frequency based on performance data.

What is the difference between organic and paid social?

Organic social builds your community and brand presence through regular content. Paid social uses targeted advertising to reach specific audiences beyond your followers. Most effective strategies combine both — organic for trust and engagement, paid for reach and conversions.

How do you measure social media success?

We track engagement rate, reach, follower growth, website traffic from social, and conversions. For paid campaigns, we measure cost per click, cost per lead, and ROAS. Monthly reports include performance trends, content analysis, and strategic recommendations.

Can you manage influencer partnerships?

Yes. We handle influencer identification, outreach, negotiation, briefing, and campaign reporting. We focus on finding creators whose audience genuinely overlaps with your target market rather than chasing follower counts.
