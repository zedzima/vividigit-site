WHY CONVERSION AND RETENTION MATTER

Acquisition Without Retention Is a Budget Leak

The average app loses 75% of users in the first week. Fixing conversion and retention is the highest-ROI work in app marketing.

Store page conversion determines acquisition efficiency

You pay for every impression — organic or paid. If your store page does not convert, every dollar spent on visibility is wasted. Screenshot strategy, icon testing, and Custom Product Pages are where conversion rate is won or lost.

1

Onboarding is the make-or-break moment

Users decide whether to stay within the first session. Onboarding optimization — first-run flows, permission prompts, value demonstration — is where retention starts. Not week two. Minute two.

2

Retention compounds — churn destroys

A 5% improvement in retention rate compounds into dramatically larger user bases over time. Conversely, high churn means you must acquire ever more users just to stay flat. Push strategy, CRM flows, and re-engagement campaigns are how sustainable apps keep users.

3

Subscription apps live or die on paywall and lifecycle

For subscription apps, conversion and retention are directly revenue. Paywall optimization, trial-to-paid flows, and subscription lifecycle management determine LTV — the metric that makes or breaks unit economics.

4

FAQ

Common Questions About App Conversion & Retention

What is app store CRO?

App store conversion rate optimization improves the percentage of store page visitors who install your app. It includes screenshot strategy, icon testing, Custom Product Pages, and A/B testing. Higher conversion means more installs from the same traffic — organic and paid.

How much does store CRO cost?

A Store CRO Sprint starts at $600 — screenshot strategy plus two A/B tests. Individual tasks like icon testing or Custom Product Pages start at $160-600. Ongoing CRO programs are available as monthly engagements.

What does a retention audit include?

Onboarding flow analysis, retention cohort diagnostics, push and CRM assessment, subscription funnel review, and a prioritized action plan. It shows where users drop off and which fixes have the biggest impact on retention and LTV.

Do you handle subscription optimization?

Yes. Paywall testing, trial-to-paid conversion, subscription lifecycle management, and pricing experiments. For subscription apps, retention is directly revenue — we optimize the entire subscription funnel.

Can you localize store page creatives?

Yes. Multilingual app store CRO across multiple languages. Localized screenshots, copy, and creative testing per market. International app store conversion optimization is a core capability.

What is the best first step?

If your store page conversion is below benchmarks: start with App Store CRO. If users install but do not retain: start with a Retention Audit. Both are one-time scopes with clear deliverables and no ongoing commitment.
