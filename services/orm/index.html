WHY ORM MATTERS

Reputation Damage Costs More to Fix Than to Prevent

One negative article, one review crisis, or one unmanaged branded search result can undo years of brand building. Online reputation management is insurance that also generates value.

Page one of Google is your brand's first impression

90% of buyers check search results before engaging with a brand. If page one shows negative reviews, outdated articles, or competitor content — that is the brand story buyers see, regardless of what your website says.

1

Reviews are a buying signal, not just feedback

For B2B buyers evaluating vendors on G2, Capterra, or Glassdoor — and for consumers checking Google Reviews or Trustpilot — review quality directly affects conversion rates, pricing power, and shortlist decisions.

2

Crisis readiness is cheaper than crisis response

Brands that have a crisis communication plan, pre-built response templates, and ongoing monitoring respond faster, limit damage, and recover sooner. Brands without this infrastructure improvise — and the cost of improvisation is measured in lost revenue and trust.

3
WHAT ORM COVERS

What Online Reputation Management Includes

Branded SERP management

Monitor and optimize what appears when someone searches your brand name. Suppress negative results through positive content creation, authority building, and technical SEO — not black-hat tricks.

Review monitoring and management

Track reviews across Google, Glassdoor, Trustpilot, G2, and industry-specific platforms. Respond professionally, escalate issues, and build a review generation system that improves ratings organically.

Crisis communication

Pre-built response plans, rapid response protocols, and stakeholder communication frameworks. Ready before the crisis happens — not assembled during it.

ORM FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask About Reputation Management

Can you remove negative content from Google?

We cannot force Google to delete third-party content. But we can suppress it by building stronger, more authoritative positive content that outranks it. For content that violates platform policies, we can pursue removal through official channels.

How long does reputation repair take?

Suppressing negative search results typically takes 3–6 months depending on the authority of the negative content. Review profile improvement is faster — meaningful changes often appear within 4–8 weeks of active management.

Is ORM the same as PR?

ORM overlaps with digital PR but has a different focus. PR builds visibility and media relationships. ORM specifically manages what appears in branded search results, review platforms, and social mentions. Both services sit inside the Brand Marketing domain.

What is the best first step?

A reputation audit: branded SERP analysis, review platform scan, and negative content inventory. It shows the current state of your online reputation and which actions will have the biggest impact.
