Why AEO Matters

AI Search Revolution

The way people search is fundamentally changing. AI-powered tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews are increasingly how consumers discover products, services, and information. If your brand is not optimized for these AI engines, you are invisible to a rapidly growing audience.

1

Beyond Keywords

Unlike traditional SEO, AEO requires understanding how large language models process and surface information. It is not just about keywords — it is about structured data, authoritative sourcing, and content formats that AI can reliably parse. Brands that adapt early gain a significant first-mover advantage in this new search paradigm.

2

Expert-Led Optimization

Our AEO services combine deep expertise in AI systems with proven digital marketing strategy. We audit how AI engines currently perceive your brand, identify gaps and opportunities, and implement optimizations that ensure you appear in AI-generated answers across platforms.

faq

Common Questions

Practical answers about AEO

How long does it take to see results from AEO?

Initial visibility improvements in AI responses typically appear within 4-8 weeks after implementation. However, AI models are retrained periodically, so full impact builds over 2-4 months as changes propagate across platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

Which AI platforms do you optimize for?

We optimize for all major AI-powered search and assistant platforms, including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot. Each platform has different citation patterns, so we tailor strategies accordingly.

Can you guarantee AI citations for my brand?

No one can guarantee specific AI citations — LLM outputs are probabilistic and change with each model update. What we can guarantee is a systematic approach that maximizes your chances: proper entity signals, structured content, and authoritative sourcing that AI models prefer to cite.

How much does AEO cost?

Our AEO packages start at $500 for a one-time AI Visibility Audit. Ongoing optimization programs range from $1,500 to $3,500 per month depending on scope. Every engagement begins with an audit so you can see the opportunity before committing to a larger program.

How is AEO different from traditional SEO?

SEO focuses on ranking in search engine results pages. AEO focuses on being cited in AI-generated answers. The tactics differ: AEO emphasizes entity clarity, structured data, and content formats that LLMs can parse, while SEO prioritizes keywords, backlinks, and technical crawlability. The best strategy combines both.
