WHY PAID SOCIAL

Paid Social Reaches Buyers Where They Spend Attention

Search captures existing demand. Paid social creates demand by putting offers in front of the right people before they search.

Platform-native strategy beats cross-posted campaigns

What works on LinkedIn does not work on TikTok. Paid social requires platform-specific creative, audience strategy, and bidding logic. Running the same campaign across platforms wastes budget and underperforms on every one of them.

1

Creative testing is the biggest performance lever

In paid social, the creative is the targeting. Ad fatigue, audience saturation, and declining CTR are all creative problems before they are budget problems. Structured creative testing is how paid social campaigns stay profitable at scale.

2

Attribution across social platforms requires independent measurement

Platform-reported conversions overcount by 20–50%. Paid social managed for real ROAS needs server-side tracking, cross-platform attribution, and incrementality testing that does not rely on the platform marking its own homework.

3

FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask About Paid Social

Which platform should I start with?

That depends on where your audience spends time and what your buying cycle looks like. B2B usually starts with LinkedIn or Meta. B2C/DTC often starts with Meta or TikTok. We recommend based on audience data, not platform preference.

Are management fees separate from ad spend?

Yes. Our packages cover strategy, creative, optimization, and reporting. Ad spend is a separate budget you control. Management fees are transparent and do not scale as a hidden percentage of spend.

How do you handle creative production?

We develop ad creative variants, run structured A/B tests, and rotate winners. For brands that need high-volume creative (especially video for TikTok), we can coordinate with your design team or produce through our network of experts.

What is the best first step?

A paid social audit: account structure review, wasted spend analysis, and attribution gap assessment. It shows where the current setup is leaking budget and which fixes will improve ROAS fastest.
