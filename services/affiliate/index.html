WHY AFFILIATE MATTERS

Pay After the Sale — Not Before

Affiliate is the only scaled acquisition channel with zero upfront media cost. The catch: most programs fail because they attract the wrong partners.

Commission-based model eliminates wasted ad spend

In affiliate marketing you pay a fixed commission or revenue share only when a sale or qualified lead is confirmed. There is no CPM, no CPC, and no budget burned on impressions that never convert. For brands with tight margins or seasonal demand, this cost structure is hard to beat.

1

Bad programs attract bad partners — structure prevents that

An unmanaged affiliate program becomes a magnet for coupon hijackers, trademark bidders, and incentivized-click farms. These partners steal attribution from organic and paid channels without generating incremental revenue. Program architecture — terms, vetting, monitoring, and enforcement — is the difference between a profit center and a margin leak.

2

The right partners compound revenue over time

Quality affiliates build evergreen content, product reviews, and comparison pages that keep sending traffic months and years after publication. Unlike paid ads that stop the moment you stop spending, a well-managed affiliate network creates a compounding organic distribution layer.

AFFILIATE FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask About Affiliate Marketing

What commission models do you recommend?

The best model depends on your business. CPA (cost per acquisition) works well for e-commerce with clear purchase events. Revenue share suits subscription businesses. Hybrid models combining a base CPA with performance bonuses attract premium partners. We design the structure based on your margins, competition, and goals.

What is the minimum investment to start an affiliate program?

Program setup typically starts at $1,500 including platform configuration, creative assets, and initial partner recruitment. Ongoing management fees are separate. You should also budget for affiliate commissions — the actual payouts to partners for sales they generate.

How long until we see results from affiliate marketing?

Expect 2-3 months to build initial traction. Recruiting quality partners, onboarding them, and allowing time for their content to rank and convert takes time. Most programs reach meaningful revenue by month 4-6, with compounding growth as the partner base expands.

How do you prevent affiliate fraud?

We use multi-layered monitoring: automated fraud detection tools, manual traffic audits, conversion pattern analysis, and strict program terms. We check for cookie stuffing, trademark bidding, incentivized traffic, and fake leads. Suspicious partners are investigated and removed promptly.

Which affiliate platforms do you work with?

We manage programs on Impact, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Awin, Rakuten, and PartnerStack. If you already have a platform, we work within it. For new programs, we recommend the best platform based on your industry, partner types, and budget.
