WHY PAID SEARCH

PPC Should Be a Governed Growth Layer, Not an Open-Ended Media Spend Habit

Search captures intent. Shopping scales product demand. Remarketing recovers missed conversions. PPC works when these layers are managed deliberately, not bundled into a vague retainer.

Fixed-fee scope logic is cleaner than percent-of-spend incentives

Most PPC agencies earn more when you spend more, even if efficiency deteriorates. This service is built around active scopes and governed delivery instead. You pay for specialist work, not for the agency's ability to let the platform burn a larger budget.

1

PPC should separate search, Shopping, and remarketing instead of blurring them

Each paid layer has different logic. Search depends on query governance. Shopping depends on feed quality and product segmentation. Remarketing depends on audience control and exclusions. A serious PPC service manages these as distinct scopes under one operating system.

2

International expansion multiplies complexity faster than it multiplies return

Every additional language and market means more campaign copies, more search behavior variation, more feed or audience complexity, and more room for waste. That is why the service includes one language and one country or region by default, then scales through explicit cart modifiers.

3

Measurement quality determines how trustworthy optimisation really is

Platform conversions are not enough. PPC needs conversion tracking, attribution checks, and reporting that can explain what is happening in business terms. Otherwise the account is only optimised against the platform's version of reality.

4

FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask About PPC Management

What is included in month one?

Month one includes audit and setup at the same monthly package rate. We diagnose the account, define the active PPC scopes, and launch or restructure the work accordingly.

How are the packages structured?

Starter covers one active PPC scope. Growth covers up to two active scopes. Scale covers up to four active scopes. The package logic is based on governed delivery capacity, not on ad spend percentage.

Do extra languages and countries change the price?

Yes. Each package includes one language and one country or region. Additional languages and markets are added in the order cart using the built-in pricing modifiers.

Are management fees separate from ad spend?

Yes. The package covers specialist work only. Ad spend, platform fees, translators, external vendors, and any required third-party tools are separate and must be approved separately.

Do you handle paid social or marketplace ads inside this PPC service?

No. This PPC service covers paid search, Shopping, Microsoft Ads, and remarketing for websites. Paid Social, App User Acquisition, and Marketplace Management are separate services.

Which PPC scopes can be combined?

The main combinations are Google Ads Search plus Remarketing, Search plus Shopping, or a broader stack that adds Microsoft Ads. The right mix depends on your funnel, catalog, and market complexity.
