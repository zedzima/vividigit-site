Why PPC Matters

Immediate Results

Pay-per-click advertising delivers immediate, measurable results. Unlike organic channels that take months to build momentum, PPC puts your brand in front of high-intent buyers the moment they search for what you offer. When managed properly, it is one of the most predictable and scalable revenue drivers in digital marketing.
1

Efficiency First

The key to PPC success is efficiency. Every wasted click is wasted budget. Our approach focuses on granular targeting, continuous bid optimization, and rigorous A/B testing to ensure your ads reach the right audience at the lowest cost per acquisition. We manage campaigns across Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and display networks with a unified strategy that maximizes total return.
2

Full Transparency

We believe in full transparency. You will see exactly where every dollar goes, which keywords and ads drive conversions, and how your campaigns compare to industry benchmarks. No hidden fees, no long-term lock-ins — just disciplined campaign management that improves every month.

faq

Common Questions

Practical answers about PPC advertising

What is the minimum budget needed for PPC?

There is no universal minimum, but we recommend at least $1,000-$2,000 per month in ad spend for meaningful results. Smaller budgets limit the data available for optimization. Our management fees are separate from your ad spend and start at $300 for a one-time audit.

How quickly will I see results from PPC campaigns?

PPC delivers traffic immediately once campaigns go live. However, optimization takes 2-4 weeks as we gather data on which keywords, ads, and audiences convert best. Most clients see significant ROAS improvements within the first 30-60 days of active management.

Which platforms do you manage?

We manage campaigns on Google Ads (Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube), Microsoft Ads, Amazon PPC, and other marketplace advertising platforms. We recommend the platform mix based on your audience and goals.

How often do you report on campaign performance?

You get access to a live dashboard with real-time metrics. We provide detailed written reports monthly, with key metric summaries weekly. For Enterprise clients, we offer bi-weekly strategy calls to review performance and adjust priorities.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. Our one-time services like audits have no ongoing commitment. For monthly management packages, we work on a month-to-month basis after an initial 3-month optimization period. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
