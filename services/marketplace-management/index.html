WHY MARKETPLACES MATTER

Marketplace Algorithms Decide Who Sells — Not Just Who Lists

Over 60% of online product searches start on Amazon, not Google. If your listings are not optimized for marketplace search, advertising, and conversion, you are invisible where buyers actually shop.

Marketplace search is a separate discipline from web SEO

Amazon A9, eBay Cassini, and Walmart search algorithms rank products by relevance, conversion rate, seller metrics, and advertising performance — not backlinks or domain authority. Keyword research, listing copy, and backend search terms follow different rules on every platform. Treating marketplace listings like website product pages leaves ranking potential on the table.

1

Buy Box ownership determines revenue — not just product quality

On Amazon, over 80% of purchases happen through the Buy Box. Winning it depends on price competitiveness, fulfillment method, seller health metrics, and inventory availability. Losing the Buy Box means losing sales to competitors selling your own product. Inventory strategy, pricing rules, and fulfillment optimization are not optional.

2

Acquisition without retention is a margin trap

Marketplace advertising costs rise every year. Brands that rely solely on PPC to drive sales end up in a bidding war with no exit strategy. Retention programs — post-purchase flows, review generation, brand registry content, and Subscribe & Save enrollment — reduce dependence on paid traffic and increase customer lifetime value.

MARKETPLACE FAQ

Questions Buyers Ask About Marketplace Management

Which marketplaces do you work with?

We manage accounts on Amazon (US, EU, UK), eBay, Walmart Marketplace, Etsy, and Shopify-powered storefronts. We also work with regional platforms like Allegro, Bol.com, and Mercado Libre depending on your target markets.

Do we need a professional seller account before starting?

Yes. For Amazon, you need a Professional Seller account ($39.99/month). For other platforms, the equivalent verified business account is required. We can guide you through account setup if you are starting from scratch.

How long before we see results from listing optimization?

Listing optimization improvements typically show in search rankings within 2-4 weeks. For new product launches, expect 4-8 weeks to build initial traction including reviews, organic ranking, and advertising data. We set specific KPIs and timelines during the strategy phase.

What advertising budget do you recommend for marketplaces?

For Amazon PPC, we typically recommend starting with $1,000-2,000/month per product category to gather meaningful data. The optimal budget depends on your category competition, margins, and growth targets. We scale spend based on ROAS performance.

Can you manage our existing marketplace accounts?

Yes. We can take over management of existing seller accounts, audit current performance, and implement improvements without disrupting your operations. We start with a Marketplace Audit to identify the highest-impact opportunities.
